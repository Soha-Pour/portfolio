Hi, I'm Soha

And I’m a User Experience Researcher with a passion for technology. That’s why I enrolled in a ten week Intro to Programming course at Epicodus code school. By the end of the course, I will have the software fluency I need to be an effective tech-based researcher. I will be able to step into the shoes of the software engineers I work with everyday. By seeing products from the perspectice of programmers, I will be a far more impactful collaborator.

I've been interesed in technology for a long time. In college, I studied cognitive science - the branch of psychology concerened with memory, perception, attention, and other functions of the mind. Often times, when people design technology, they don't think much about how the end user, a human being, will use it. Through studying psychology and software engineering, I hope to bridge the gap between (wo)man and machine.
